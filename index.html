About Us

Welcome to the Robotics Club of SRMCEM, where innovation meets engineering. We are passionate about exploring the limitless possibilities of robotics, from autonomous systems to human-robot interaction. Our club is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology and fostering a love for all things mechanical and electronic.

Join us in shaping the future of robotics and making a difference in the world. Let's explore, learn, and create together!
